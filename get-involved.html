Get Involved with Stratford PAL

Volunteer your time, donate, sponsor a program, or bring your community connections. Every contribution — large or small — makes a real difference.

Volunteer

Give your time and skills to make our programs possible

Collaboration of community leaders and volunteers with Stratford Police Officers is what makes PAL flourish. Whether you want to lead a class, coach a sport, assist with events, or share a skill — there's a place for you here.

We invite anyone with an idea for a new program to reach out. All ages are welcome to bring their ideas and let's put something together!

1
Email us with your name, availability, and what you'd like to contribute
2
We'll schedule a brief conversation to find the right fit
3
Get oriented and start making a difference right away

Donate

100% of every donation goes directly to programs

Every dollar donated to Stratford PAL is used directly for the benefit of our programs. No overhead drain, no administrative black holes — your generosity goes straight to the youth we serve.

Donations fund scholarships, equipment grants, field trips, supplies, and new program development. Your contribution has an immediate, tangible impact on real young people in our community.

1
Click the Donate button below to visit our secure donation page
2
Choose a one-time or recurring donation amount
3
Your contribution funds programs immediately

Volunteer Opportunities

No matter your skills or schedule, there's a meaningful way to contribute to Stratford PAL. Here are some of the ways community volunteers get involved.

🏋️

Program Instruction

Teach or assist with karate, baking, chess, fishing, or any skill you have to share.

📋

Event Coordination

Help organize, plan, and run PAL events, registration days, and community outings.

🌱

Garden & Maintenance

Help tend the PAL garden for the Roots & Recipes program — planting, watering, harvesting.

📱

Social Media & Outreach

Help spread the word about PAL programs and events through social media and community channels.

🎒

Summer Camp Support

Assist with summer camp operations, field trips, and youth supervision during July camps.

💡

New Program Ideas

Have a skill, hobby, or connection that could become a PAL program? Reach out — we're always listening.
