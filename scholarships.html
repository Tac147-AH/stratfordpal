Access for All

Scholarships & Financial Assistance

Cost should never be a barrier. Stratford PAL is committed to making our programs accessible to every youth in our community — regardless of financial circumstance.

Our Commitment

No Youth Left Behind

Stratford PAL was built on the belief that every young person in our community deserves access to quality programs, positive adult mentorship, and opportunities to grow — regardless of family income.

Through community donations, grant funding, and partnerships with local organizations, we work hard to keep our programs affordable or fully subsidized for families who need it most.

💛
If cost is a concern for your family, please reach out. We will do everything we can to make participation possible. Your child's involvement in PAL is what matters most to us.

How We Help

Three Ways We Keep Programs Accessible

🎓

Scholarship Assistance

Families experiencing financial hardship can inquire about scholarship support for any Stratford PAL program. We handle these conversations with care and discretion. Simply reach out — no formal application required.

Equipment Provided

For programs like PAL Hockey, grant funding allows us to provide full equipment to youth who need it. No family should have to choose between gearing up and participating. We've got you covered.

Scholarship FAQs
