# Stratford PAL — Sitemap & Site Architecture

## Page Hierarchy

```
stratfordpal.org (root)
├── index.html          → Home
├── about.html          → About Us
├── programs.html       → Our Programs (all 9)
├── events.html         → Events & Calendar
├── scholarships.html   → Scholarships & Financial Assistance
├── get-involved.html   → Get Involved (Volunteer, Donate, Sponsor, Partner)
└── contact.html        → Contact Us
```

## Internal Link Strategy
- Every page links to Programs, Events, Scholarships, Contact
- Every program card links to its anchor on programs.html
- "Donate" always links to Donorbox external page
- "Volunteer Application" links to Google Form
- Social media links to Facebook and Instagram
- "Get notified" flows route to contact or email
