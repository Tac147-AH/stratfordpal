Police Cadets & Explorers

Work alongside Stratford Police Officers to develop real leadership skills and community pride

👤 Ages 13–20 (Junior: Middle School)
📅 Year-round enrollment
📍 PAL Building, 900 Longbrook Ave

The Police Cadet/Explorer program gives youth and young adults ages 13–20 the opportunity to work directly with Stratford Police Officers. Through hands-on experiences, participants develop leadership, self-confidence, and respect for others.

The Junior Cadet program is tailored specifically for Stratford Middle School students, offering age-appropriate curriculum and activities that set a strong foundation for the Explorer program.

Both programs provide a unique insight into law enforcement careers while focusing on the core values of service, integrity, and leadership. Registration is open year-round.

PAL Karate

Year-round karate for all ages and skill levels — from beginners to competitors

👤 Ages 3 and up
📅 Tuesdays, 7:00–8:00 PM
📍 PAL Building, 900 Longbrook Ave
🔄 Year-round

PAL Karate is a year-round program held every Tuesday evening from 7–8 PM at the PAL Building. Open to participants ages 3 and up, this class welcomes all skill levels from complete beginners to those looking to compete.

Our experienced instructor provides a structured curriculum that builds physical discipline, mental focus, and respect — essential skills both on and off the mat. Students who advance have the opportunity to attend and compete in local tournaments.

Whether your child is picking up martial arts for the first time or continuing their training, PAL Karate offers a welcoming and supportive environment to grow.

PAL Hockey

Learn to Skate and Learn to Play Hockey — super low-cost and fully equipped

👤 Youth
📅 Summer · Skating begins July
📍 Shelton Sports Center
📋 Registration opens May

With the generous support of The Rinks at Shelton at the Shelton Sports Center, Stratford PAL offers super low-cost Learn to Skate and Learn to Play Hockey programs each summer.

Sgt. Albohn and Detective Creatore built this program from the ground up — including securing grant funding to outfit up to 60 hockey players with equipment annually. No youth is turned away due to lack of gear.

Registration opens each May, with skating beginning in July. Whether your child has never been on the ice or is ready to learn the game, this program provides a safe, fun, and affordable entry into hockey.

Baking Workshops

Hands-on baking for all ages — sessions vary in length, difficulty, and focus

👤 All ages
📅 Throughout the year
📍 PAL Building, 900 Longbrook Ave

Baking workshops at Stratford PAL are held throughout the year and vary in length and difficulty, offering sessions for participants of all ages. From beginner-friendly sessions to more advanced baking challenges, there's something for everyone.

Summer Camps

A Stratford PAL tradition — fun-filled summer camps with field trips and lasting memories

👤 Youth
📅 July annually
📍 PAL Building, 900 Longbrook Ave
📋 Registration opens May

Summer Camps are a beloved staple of Stratford PAL — run every year out of the PAL Building, they provide youth with an unforgettable summer experience filled with activities and fun field trips.

Camps run annually in July, with registration opening in May. Spots are limited and fill up fast, so be sure to sign up early when registration opens. Follow us on social media for announcements.

These camps aren't just about keeping kids busy during summer — they're about building friendships, creating memories, and connecting youth with positive role models in a safe and supported environment.

COPfit Wellness Program

A 4-week officer-developed wellness and fitness program for youth — spots fill fast!

👤 Youth
📅 Summer · 4 weeks
📍 PAL Building, 900 Longbrook Ave
📋 Registration opens May

COPfit is a wellness, fitness, and exercise program developed specifically by Stratford Police Officers for our community's youth. This 4-week summer program combines physical activity with wellness education in a dynamic, officer-led environment.

The program runs during the summer and consistently fills up fast — so if you're interested, make sure to watch for registration opening in May and sign up right away.

COPfit isn't just about fitness — it's about building healthy habits, positive relationships with officers, and a foundation of wellness that serves youth for years to come.

Bike Squad

Bike safety, skills, and community adventure — led by Stratford Police Officers

👤 Youth
📅 May through September
📍 Local routes, Stratford area

The Bike Squad was formed to give youth in our community a dedicated resource for learning bike safety, developing their riding abilities, and getting outdoors. Led by a dedicated team of officers from the Stratford Police Department representing all skill levels.

The program runs from May through September each year, taking participants on rides and adventures around the area. It's a great combination of outdoor activity, skill development, and positive officer interaction.

Join the Bike Squad and ride with the officers to your next community adventure!

Roots & Recipes

Farm-to-table gardening and healthy cooking — a true one-of-a-kind program

👤 Ages 6–18
📅 Summer program
📍 PAL Building Garden, 900 Longbrook Ave
📋 Registration opens March

Roots and Recipes is a one-of-a-kind program where youth ages 6–18 grow their own food in the PAL Building's garden alongside police officers, then use that harvest to create healthy recipes.

Participants learn all aspects of gardening — from planting seedlings to harvesting crops — and develop a genuine understanding of healthy living and nutrition. The program culminates in cooking and enjoying meals made from ingredients they grew themselves.

A massive hit in 2025, this farm-to-table program will run again with registration opening in March, just before seedlings are started. Don't miss your chance to be part of this extraordinary experience!
