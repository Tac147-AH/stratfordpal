Common Questions

Before You Reach Out

📋

How do I register?

Email spal@townofstratford.com with your name, your child's name, age, and the program you're interested in. We'll respond with next steps.

💰

What's the cost?

Costs vary by program. Many are very low-cost or free. Financial assistance is available — just ask. Email us and we'll give you full details for any specific program.

📅

When does registration open?

Most summer programs open registration in May. Year-round programs like Karate and Police Cadets accept registration anytime. Follow our social media for announcements.
