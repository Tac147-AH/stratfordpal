Our Mission

More Than Sports — We're Building a Community

The Stratford Police Department believes we must do more than enforce the law. Through PAL, we build meaningful relationships between officers and youth — creating a safer, more united Stratford.

We focus on youth enrichment, education, and leadership — not just athletics. Every dollar raised goes directly to benefit the many programs we run for our community.
