Our Story

About Stratford PAL

We are law enforcement officers and community leaders united by one goal: the positive development of Stratford's youth.

Who We Are

Youth Focused.
Community Committed.

Stratford PAL is an organization of law enforcement officers and community leaders who work together towards the positive development of our youth.

The Stratford Police Department recognizes that we must do more than just enforce the law. We strive to build and maintain positive relationships with the people we serve — especially our youth. We believe that strong police-community relations will make Stratford a more united and safer community.

"We strongly believe that PAL should not just focus on sports, but on youth enrichment, education and leadership."

Every dollar we raise is used directly for the benefit of the many programs sponsored by our organization. We are deeply grateful to the community donors, volunteers, and partner organizations who make this work possible.

Our Commitment

Building bridges between law enforcement and the community through authentic relationships and shared experiences.

Core Principles

How We Show Up for Our Community

Everything we do is guided by a deep commitment to the families and youth we serve in Stratford and neighboring communities.

🤝

Build Trust

We create genuine connections between law enforcement officers and the youth and families they serve — fostering mutual respect and understanding.

🌟

Empower Youth

Through programs that develop leadership, fitness, life skills, and confidence, we help young people discover their potential and build lasting character.

🏘️

Serve the Community

We're rooted in Stratford and open to neighboring communities. Our doors are open to all — and we actively seek out partnerships to expand our reach.

Our Journey

How We've Grown

From a vision to strengthen police-community bonds, Stratford PAL has grown into a multi-program youth organization that serves hundreds of families across the region.

Founding

Stratford PAL Established

Stratford Police Department launches PAL with a mission to go beyond law enforcement — building positive relationships with youth through sports and activities.

Early Programs

Core Programs Launched

PAL Karate and Police Cadets/Explorers programs established as year-round offerings for Stratford youth.

Growth

Summer Programs Expand

Summer Camps, COPfit Wellness, and PAL Hockey launched with support from Shelton Sports Center and grant funding to equip players.

2025

New Programs Debut

Roots & Recipes (farm-to-table gardening and cooking) and Fishing Club added, along with continued growth of the Bike Squad program.

Today

9+ Active Programs

Stratford PAL now runs 9+ programs year-round, serving youth from ages 3 to 20 and their families throughout Stratford and neighboring communities.

Our Volunteers

Powered by Community

Stratford PAL would not exist without the incredible community leaders, volunteers, and officers who give their time to make our programs possible.
